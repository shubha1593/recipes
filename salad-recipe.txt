Ingredients:
	- 1 1/2 cups cooked rajma (kidney beans)
	- 1 1/2 cups cooked fresh corn (preferably sweet corn)
	- 1 small onion, chopped
	- salt and black pepper (kalimirch) powder to taste
	- Vinaigrette Dressing or Low Calorie Dressing

Directions:
	1. Mix all the salad ingredients in a bowl.
	2. Put in the refrigerator.
	3. Just before serving, add the dressing and toss.
	4. Serve cold.
